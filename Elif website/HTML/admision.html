Admissions Process

Your future begins here—apply online and join Elif Pre-University College.

How to Apply

Elif Pre-University College invites applications from students who are ready to thrive in a dynamic academic environment. Our online admissions system is designed to be simple, secure, and accessible to families across Somalia.

Application Steps

  1. Fill Out Student Information: Provide the learner’s full name, date of birth, grade level to enroll (Pre-K to 12th Grade), and previous school (if applicable).
  2. Enter Parent/Guardian Details: Include the full name, email address, and phone number of the parent or guardian responsible for the application.
  3. Upload Application Documents: Submit the learner’s birth certificate, previous school records, or other relevant documents in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format.
  4. Submit the Application: Once all fields are completed and documents uploaded, click “Submit Application” to send your form to our admissions team.

Important Dates

  • Applications Open: October 1st
  • Application Deadline: January 31st
  • Admission Results: March 15th
