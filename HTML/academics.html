Our Curriculum

Fostering a lifelong love of learning and intellectual curiosity.

Our academic program is designed to challenge students at every level, from foundational learning in our Lower School to advanced college-preparatory courses in our Upper School. We emphasize critical thinking, collaboration, and real-world problem-solving.

Our Lower School builds a strong foundation in core subjects like reading, writing, and mathematics. Through project-based learning and interactive activities, we nurture curiosity and help young students discover the joy of learning in a supportive environment.

In Middle School, students transition to a more departmentalized structure, exploring subjects like advanced sciences, humanities, world languages, and the arts in greater depth. We focus on developing organizational skills, independent study habits, and responsible digital citizenship.

The Upper School offers a rigorous, college-preparatory curriculum designed to challenge students and prepare them for success in higher education and beyond. A wide selection of Honors and Advanced Placement (AP) courses are available, alongside specialized tracks in STEM, fine arts, and global studies.

Elif Pre-University College is dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth in a supportive, dynamic learning environment. Join us to shape the future.
